Weekly Short Story Discussions - வாராந்திர சிறுகதை கலந்துரையாடல்

Every Wednesday at 8:00 PM EST, the Anjirai Literary Circle brings readers together for an engaging and insightful exploration of Tamil short stories

Each session focuses on a thoughtfully selected short story. Participants collectively examine the narrative craft, literary devices, themes, character arcs, and the writer’s artistic choices. The discussions encourage both appreciation and critical thinking, offering space for constructive analysis and deeper understanding.

More than a meeting, this is a community experience. Anjirai’s weekly discussions create a warm and inspiring environment where Tamil literature is celebrated, imagination is sparked, and meaningful connections are built through shared dialogue
